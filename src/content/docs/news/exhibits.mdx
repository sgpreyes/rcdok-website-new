During this Lenten season, different parishes, chapels present exhibitions of images of Christ’s passion along with the saints we usually see during Holy Week processions.

Here are the list of the ongoing and future exhibits in the Diocese:

Dakilang Pag-ibig: Mga Tagpo sa Pagpapakasakit ng Panginoon
Diocesan Shrine and Parish of Immaculate Conception – Malabon
March 16 to March 25 | 9:00am to 9:00pm

“Pag-ibig Mo’y ipakita, iligtas Mo kami sa dusa” Pabasa Caridad and Lenten Exhibit
Divine Mercy Spiritual Renewal Center – Malabon
March 17 to 18 | 11:00am onwards

Panata: Mga Tagpo sa Pagpapakasakit ni Kristo
Our Lady of Most Holy Rosary Chapel – SM Grand Central
March 27 to April 10 | Mall hours

Recently, Sto. Niño de Pasión Parish in Navotas, and Sts. Peter and John Parish in Malabon concluded their exhibit.
