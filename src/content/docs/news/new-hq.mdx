Diocese of Kalookan Curia officials, along with mission partners, inaugurated St. Therese Mission Headquarters in Malabon City, March 31, 2023.

Kalookan Bishop Pablo Virgilio David blessed the structure.

Construction of the headquarters, which includes a function hall and three bedrooms for missionaries, began in May 2022 under the supervision of the Office of Vicar for Properties.

The property on which the headquarters is situated was generously donated by spouses Carlos and Emelinda Reyes, and Edgardo and Inocencia Eugenio Sr.

The mission headquarters is named after Therese of Lisieux. She was declared the patroness of missions by Pope Pius XI in 1927.
