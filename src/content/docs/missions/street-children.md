# Center of Mercy for Street Children

<div style="margin-bottom:2rem; padding:1rem; border-radius:12px; background-color:#f9f9f9; box-shadow:0 4px 6px rgba(0,0,0,0.05);">
  <p style="font-size:1.1rem; line-height:1.6; color:#333;">
    Located at San Roque Cathedral Compound, A. Mabini St., Caloocan City, the Center of Mercy for Street Children provides street children a safe place to read, study, eat meals, bathe, and receive Catechesis on Catholic teachings.
