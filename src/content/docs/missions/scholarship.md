# Youth Servant Leadership and Education Program (YSLEP)

<div style="margin-bottom:2rem; padding:1rem; border-radius:12px; background-color:#f9f9f9; box-shadow:0 4px 6px rgba(0,0,0,0.05);">
  <p style="font-size:1.1rem; line-height:1.6; color:#333;">
    YSLEP is the flagship program of CARITAS MANILA with other Implementing Caritas Partners (ICP). It aims to break the chains of poverty by providing college education opportunities for deserving youth in the Diocese of Kalookan.
  </p>
</div>

---

## Vision
<div style="margin-bottom:1rem; padding:1rem; border-left:4px solid #4CAF50; background-color:#f0fdf4;">
  Developed Servant Leaders of their Community with Self-discipline, Moral Integrity, and Social Responsibility.
</div>

## Mission
<div style="margin-bottom:2rem; padding:1rem; border-left:4px solid #2196F3; background-color:#e8f4fd;">
  <ol style="margin-left:1.5rem; line-height:1.6; color:#333;">
    <li>Prioritize disadvantaged youth from the Diocese of Kalookan</li>
    <li>Guide partners to complete the program</li>
    <li>Inspire partners on Stewardship values</li>
  </ol>
</div>

---

## Program Details
<div style="margin-bottom:2rem; padding:1rem; border-radius:12px; background-color:#fff8e1;">
  YSLEP integrates academic and leadership development for scholastic excellence and Christian values formation.
  
  <p style="font-weight:600; margin-top:1rem;">12 Priority Beneficiaries:</p>
  <ul style="margin-left:1.5rem; line-height:1.6;">
    <li>Urban Poor Youth</li>
    <li>Menial Youth Workers</li>
    <li>Children in Conflict with the Law</li>
    <li>Children of Fishermen and Farmers</li>
    <li>Differently Abled Youth</li>
    <li>Orphans</li>
    <li>Young Indigenous People</li>
    <li>Children and Youth Survivors of War and Disasters</li>
    <li>Children and Youth Survivors of Human Trafficking</li>
    <li>Children of Prisoners</li>
    <li>Street Children and Youth</li>
    <li>High School and College Dropouts</li>
  </ul>
</div>

---

## Major Activities
<div style="margin-bottom:2rem; padding:1rem; border-radius:12px; background-color:#f0f4f8;">
  <ul style="margin-left:1.5rem; line-height:1.6; color:#333;">
    <li>Screening applicants prior to the final interview of Caritas Manila YSLEP Team</li>
    <li>Monthly YSL Formation Training – Cycles 1,2,3 & 4</li>
    <li>Monthly Monitoring, Evaluating & Reporting</li>
    <li>Planning and Structuring of Formations/Training</li>
    <li>Inter-diocesan coordinators’ meeting</li>
    <li>Livelihood Training with parents of YSLEP scholars</li>
    <li>Cooperative values integration</li>
    <li>Resource mobilization integration</li>
    <li>Empowering scholars</li>
    <li>Networking and collaboration</li>
    <li>Coordinating Church and Social activities for scholars</li>
  </ul>
</div>
