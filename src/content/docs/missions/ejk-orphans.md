# EJK ORPHANS PROGRAM

<div style="margin-bottom: 2rem; padding:1rem; border-radius:12px; background-color:#f9f9f9; box-shadow:0 4px 6px rgba(0,0,0,0.05);">
  <p style="font-size:1.1rem; line-height:1.6; color:#333;">
    The shepherd of the Diocese of Kalookan, <strong>Bishop Pablo “Ambo” Virgilio S. David, D.D.</strong>, has initiated support for orphaned children and widowed wives of EJK victims. Spiritual, social, and economic assistance is provided to help families seek justice for their loved ones.
  </p>
</div>

---

## Assistance to PWDs / PWSNs

<div style="margin-bottom: 2rem; padding:1rem; border-left: 4px solid #4CAF50; background-color:#f0fdf4;">
  <p style="font-size:1.05rem; line-height:1.6; color:#333;">
    In partnership with the Dominican Sisters of the Most Holy Rosary, the Diocese launched programs for <strong>persons with disabilities (PWD)</strong> and <strong>persons with special needs (PWSN)</strong> in December 2018. The program focuses on:
  </p>
  <ul style="margin-left:1.5rem; color:#333;">
    <li>Skills development</li>
    <li>Stewardship values</li>
    <li>Resource mobilization</li>
  </ul>
</div>
