# Salubong Rehabilitation Program

<div style="margin-bottom:2rem; padding:1rem; border-radius:12px; background-color:#f9f9f9; box-shadow:0 4px 6px rgba(0,0,0,0.05);">
  <p style="font-size:1.1rem; line-height:1.6; color:#333;">
    “Task Force Salubong” is a project of the Diocese for the six-month rehabilitation of persons with substance abuse disorder, providing psychological orientation that is family and community based. Participants are also taught livelihood programs so that they can return to normal life in society.
  </p>

  </p>
</div>
