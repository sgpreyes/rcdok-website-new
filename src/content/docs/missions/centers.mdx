### Tulay ng Kabataan

<MissionProfile 
  name="Tulay ng Kabataan" 
  img={anakImg}
  fbLink="https://www.facebook.com/anaktnkfoundation/"
  category="Social Welfare and Development Organization"
  address="94 Kalayaan Ave., Brgy. Central, Quezon City"
  contact="contact@anak-tnk.org"
>
  For the past 25 years, ANAK-Tnk has been working with the poorest and most vulnerable people
  in Metro Manila, Philippines: children, abandoned people in the streets and families in the slums. 
  The priority of ANAK-Tnk is the care of each and every child to help them rebuild their lives
  despite their deep wounds. The foundation’s actions are based on a family and lasting commitment.

  <div style={{ marginTop: '1.5rem' }}>
    
    <details style={accordionStyle}>
      <summary style={summaryStyle}>01. Nutrition</summary>
      <div style={contentStyle}>
      In the Philippines, more than one in ten children do not have enough to eat and one in three is stunted.
      We provide balanced meals to children and training in nutrition to local poor communities.
      </div>
    </details>

    <details style={accordionStyle}>
      <summary style={summaryStyle}>02. Education</summary>
      <div style={contentStyle}>
        In the Philippines, one child out of ten is not attending school. We support each child throughout
        his or her schooling, which we adapt according to his or her needs.
      </div>
    </details>

    <details style={accordionStyle}>
      <summary style={summaryStyle}>03. Health</summary>
      <div style={contentStyle}>
      40% of the urban population lives in extreme unhealthy living conditions. We provide each child with
      appropriate medical care and train local poor communities in basic hygiene and health.
      </div>
    </details>

    <details style={accordionStyle}>
      <summary style={summaryStyle}>04. Protection</summary>
      <div style={contentStyle}>
      Drugs, violence, rape, prostitution: children are the first victims. We provide psychological and
      legal support based on our prevention and child protection Charter.
      </div>
    </details>

  </div>
</MissionProfile>

### Philbook

<MissionProfile 
  name="Philbook" 
  img={phbkImg}
  fbLink="https://www.facebook.com/profile.php?id=100094386333503"
  category="Social Welfare and Development Organization"
  address="Blk 2, Pamasawata C3, Dagat-Dagatan, Caloocan"
  contact="ig: @philbookngo"
>
  Philbook in Caloocan is a non-profit organization focused on improving children's access to culture and
  education. They organize community activities, such as field trips and holiday parties for local children,
  often featuring French volunteers who aim to promote these children’s development. 

  <div style={{ marginTop: '1.5rem' }}>
    
    <details style={accordionStyle}>
      <summary style={summaryStyle}>01. Street Libraries</summary>
      <div style={contentStyle}>
      We organized street libraries from Tuesday to Sunday. During these sessions, a story was read by a volunteer
      Philippine. Once the story was finished, the children received a coloring page and could participate in an 
      activity with various logic, French, or math games that we had prepared beforehand.
      </div>
    </details>

    <details style={accordionStyle}>
      <summary style={summaryStyle}>02. Fixed library</summary>
      <div style={contentStyle}>
        After extensive renovations, the library has become a place where children can learn while having fun. We've 
        implemented activities linked to specific skills (for example, math on Thursdays). Once the two activities 
        are completed, we read them a story, and the children can then enjoy some reading time.
      </div>
    </details>

    <details style={accordionStyle}>
      <summary style={summaryStyle}>03. Cultural events</summary>
      <div style={contentStyle}>
      We organized an Olympics for about a hundred children, a Christmas party for 200 children from the slums, and 
      a visit to the Planetarium. In addition, we coached a football team and organized matches with children from 
      other slums.
      </div>
    </details>
  </div>
</MissionProfile>
