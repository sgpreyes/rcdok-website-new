It is dedicated to San Roque, the patron saint of health and protection against plagues. The parish has a long-standing history and is essential to the community’s spiritual journey. It provides parishioners with sacramental services, religious instruction, and daily masses. The church building’s straightforward yet significant design features reflect traditional Filipino church architecture. A major component of the parish’s mission is community outreach programs, which help the underprivileged and disenfranchised. Additionally, the parish actively takes part in Navotas’ cultural and municipal events. It still encourages loyalty, empathy, and moral leadership in the neighborhood.
