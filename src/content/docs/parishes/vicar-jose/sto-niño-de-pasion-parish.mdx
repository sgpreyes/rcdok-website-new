Sto. Niño De Pasion Parish is dedicated to the Holy Child Jesus, a symbol of innocence, hope, and divine protection. It runs catechism programs, youth ministries, and retreats to deepen the faith of its members.The parish is actively engaged in charitable programs, helping marginalized groups and promoting social justice. Local festivals and the feast of Sto. Niño are celebrated with colorful processions and community gatherings. The parish fosters devotion, prayer, and moral values among parishioners. It serves as a guiding center for spiritual growth, community unity, and faith-based service.
