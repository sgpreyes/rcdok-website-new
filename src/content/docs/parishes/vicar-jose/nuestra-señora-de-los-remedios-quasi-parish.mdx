Nuestra Señora de los Remedios Quasi Parish is a Roman Catholic community located in Brgy. Tanza, Navotas City, and functions under the jurisdiction of the Diocese of Kalookan. As a "quasi parish," it serves as a spiritual hub for the local residents providing essential pastoral care, sacramental life, and social outreach under the patronage of Our Lady of Remedies. The community is characterized by its resilient faith and active devotion, often serving as a center for local religious festivities and communal support in one of Metro Manila’s most densely populated coastal areas.
